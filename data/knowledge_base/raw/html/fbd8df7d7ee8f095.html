International Students - Questions & Answers
Question 1: What should international applicants prepare in the application package?

No, they can’t.

Question 2: How long does it take to apply for a visa sponsored by ULIS?

It takes 10 - 14 days after the acceptance letter is issued.

Question 3: Where can international students get their visas?

International students can choose to receive visa at either
+ Noibai International Airport/checkpoints along Vietnam’s borders upon arrival or;
+ Vietnamese Embassies and Consulates in foreign countries before departure.

Question 4: Can full-time undergraduate students receive multiple entry visa?
Yes, they can.
Question 5: Can international students stay in ULIS Dormitory?

Since the vacancy is limited, international students need to apply for accommodation at the same time as they apply for the university.

 

Question 6: Can international students enroll in the University without providing proof of B2 Level Vietnamese?

No, they can’t.

International Specialists & Teachers - Questions & Answers
Question 1: Which forms of support do you offer to foreign specialists/teachers?

Please refer to the International Experts & Teachers to prepare your dossier.

Question 2: How do we prepare paperwork for working in ULIS?

Please refer to the International Experts & Teachers to prepare your dossier.

Question 3: Do you offer any support for my family members when I work at the University?

Our university offers help with the visa application for your family members once you have been accepted as a teacher/specialist. In such case, please provide documents which prove your familial relationship. This document must be certified by your home country, authenticated by Vietnamese consulates and translated into Vietnamese (2 notarised copies).
