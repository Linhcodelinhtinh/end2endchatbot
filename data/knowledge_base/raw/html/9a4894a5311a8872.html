      The Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science respectfully introduces information about the Vietnamese language teaching team for foreigners.
       
         NGUYỄN Dương Liễu    
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      Ms. Liễu is a highly experienced Vietnamese language teacher with an extensive academic background. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Literature and a Master's Degree in Vietnamese Studies from Vietnam National University, Hanoi. Over the past 25 years, Ms. Liễu has demonstrated her passion and commitment to teaching Vietnamese language and culture to foreigners, particularly Korean and Japanese learners of various ages, ranging from recent graduates to the elderly. Known for her ability to connect with students from diverse backgrounds, Ms. Liễu effectively bridges linguistic and cultural gaps. Her exceptional combination of patience, diligence, flexibility, and resourcefulness enables her to support learners in achieving their language goals, adapting to individual needs. Ms. Liễu, with great enthusiasm and confidence, excels at creating engaging and personalized learning environments, inspiring students to embrace the Vietnamese language and culture.

      Contact: nguyenduonglieu@gmail.com
       
       PHẠM Thị Thương Thương
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      Thương Thương has been teaching Vietnamese to international students for more than 20 years at VNU-IVIDES. She graduated from Vietnam National University, Hanoi with a bachelor's degree majoring in linguistics. Then she took up a Master's degree in Vietnamese Studies. Teaching Vietnamese language has always been her passion. She is willing to teach students of all levels. Along with building and transmitting knowledge to students,  her classes are always fun and memorable. She is looking forward to seeing you in her classes. 

      Contact: thuongthuongpham@gmail.com
         
       LÊ Thị Hoài Dương
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      Lê Hoài Dương is our dedicated Vietnamese language teacher. She holds a master's degree in Linguistics and has over 15 years of teaching experience. She is passionate about guiding you on your journey to mastering this beautiful language. She specializes in catering to all levels of learners, from beginners eager to grasp the fundamentals to advanced students aiming for fluency. Her approach is versatile, offering a range of options including conversational classes, enriching cultural workshops, and test preparations for standardized language exams. She understands that each learner has unique needs and is committed to tailoring the schedule to individual requirements. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to enhance your existing skills, she will work with you to achieve your language goals.

      Contact: leduonghoaile@gmail.com
       
       LÊ Thuý Hằng
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      LÊ Thuý Hằng holds a Ph.D. in Philology, and she has been working for more than 10 years at the Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science - Vietnam National University, Hanoi. She also holds various teaching certifications, such as TVSOL (Teaching Vietnamese Speakers of Other Languages); Innovative Educators; New Teaching Methods and Application of Information Technology in Education.
      She loves teaching Vietnamese, and she is very patient and enthusiastic. Her lessons are up-to-date and flexible, including not just language skills but also insights into Vietnamese culture. She has experience in teaching both face-to-face and online.

      Contact: lethuyhang.sphn@gmail.com
       
          NGUYỄN Thị Huệ
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      NGUYỄN Thị Huệ is a cheerful and friendly Vietnamese teacher. She holds a Ph.D. in Vietnamese Studies, having dedicated 5 years to her doctoral degree, and possesses over 10 years of experience teaching Vietnamese language and culture. She eagerly supports learners at all levels in their Vietnamese language journey. Employing various modern teaching methodologies, such as visualization, conversational learning, and field trips, she enables learners to enhance their listening, speaking, and communication skills in Vietnamese with confidence. Her goal is to impart essential knowledge and foster engaging experiences related to the Vietnamese language, culture, and people to her students.

      Contact: huent.vnh@gmail.com

       
       PHÙNG Thị Thanh Lâm
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      Dr. Lâm has experience teaching the Vietnamese Language and Culture to students from various countries around the world. She obtained an MA and BA in Linguistics and a PhD in Vietnamese Studies from  Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU). In the academic year 2023, Lam is a visiting assistant professor of Vietnamese Language at Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, Korea. With her teaching experience, Lam and her colleagues contributed to a textbook on the Vietnamese Language for foreigners which has been considered an outstanding textbook both in Vietnam and in international universities from 2004 to the present. With her tutoring and encouragement, students will develop their knowledge of the Vietnamese Language and Culture with confidence and happiness.

      Contact: phungthanhlam@gmail.com
       
       PHẠM Thị Ngọc Anh
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      Ngọc Anh is a cheerful Vietnamese teacher with over 5 years of experience teaching international students  at the Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science under Vietnam National University, Hanoi. Her background is Vietnamese Studies, and she teaches Vietnamese at elementary, intermediary and advanced levels. Besides teaching language, she is very interested in sharing and guiding foreigners about Vietnamese culture. She is very friendly, enthusiastic, helpful, and persistent in teaching and working with students, aiming to help them achieve their objectives when studying the Vietnamese language at different levels. Welcome all of you to her Vietnamese classes.

      Contact: phamngocanh120191@gmail.com
       
       VƯƠNG Thị Thuý An
      VNU-Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science
      An is a great teacher with more than 4 years of experience in teaching Vietnamese to international students at the Institute of Vietnamese Studies and Development Science under Vietnam National University, Hanoi. Her major is Vietnamese Cultural History. She finds joy in teaching international students about Vietnamese language and culture from basic to advanced levels. An believes that an interacting and engaging teaching method will help students understand and remember lessons easily. Her enthusiasm motivates her to support students in achieving their goals, regardless of their current levels.

      Contact: anvuonghd@gmail.com
